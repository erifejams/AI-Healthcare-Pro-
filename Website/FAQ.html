Frequently Asked Questions

Mental Health Related Questions

What is mental health?
Mental health includes our well being, emotional state and psychological state. It affects our behaviour, how we think, feel and respond.
Who does mental health issues affect?
Mental health issues can affect anybody.
What are the causes of mental health issues?
Causes of mental health issues include (but are not limited to the following):
  • Stress
  • Trauma
  • Depression
  • Abuse
  • Drug and alcohol misuse
  • Bullying
  • Bereavement
Where can I find more information?
More information can be found on lots of mental health organisations websites. They also provide associated services and help if you need.
Who can I contact if I have an issue regarding my mental health?
It is better to contact a professional. A list of mental health organisations can be found in the mental health orgaisation section. It is advised to contact those organisations.

InTa Related Questions

What is the full version of InTa?
InTa is the shorten version of Interactive Talker.
What are the features of InTa?
The following features are:
  • Chating with the chatbot
  • Sentiment Analysis of conversation
  • Voice Technology enabled
Why was InTa created?
InTa was created to help people with mental health issues have someone to talk to when they need. It is not a replacement of an actual therapist.
What technologies does InTa use?
  • Tensorflow
  • NLTK
  • Python
Can InTa replace me going to a mental health professional?
No. InTa is not and does not act as replacement. Instead it is highly recommended to seek help from professionals and more information can be found on our mental health organisation page.
